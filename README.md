## 🌟 About That's Mandarin

[That's Mandarin](https://www.thatsmandarin.com) was **founded in 2005 in Beijing** and has grown into one of the world's leading Chinese language schools, with campuses across China and a global student base.

**Mission:** To make learning Chinese **effective, enjoyable, and accessible** to everyone — by combining modern technology with immersive teaching methods.

## 🧠 How That's Mandarin Teaches Differently

### 1. 💻 NihaoCafe – Proprietary Learning Platform
A fully in-house built online platform (originally launched as "Mandarin Café" in 2010, rebuilt and rebranded in 2020) that allows students to study anytime, anywhere — with integrated lessons, class notes, and progress tracking.

### 2. 🔗 Chinese Link Words
A unique memorisation technique that helps students retain vocabulary **faster and more effectively** than traditional methods (previously known as "Smart Lingo").

### 3. 📖 Storytelling Techniques
Grammar and sentence structures are taught through **engaging, real-life stories**, making learning more intuitive and enjoyable.

